01 Financial performance

Revenue is not separately disclosed under the confidential filing. The company reports framework orders exceeding RMB500m across three sectors, including a three-year, 2,000-unit PM01 order from Duolun Technology worth over RMB100m. Total funding is roughly US$339m: about RMB1bn (US$139m) raised across rounds in late 2025 plus the US$200m Series B in April 2026. Production targets rise from about 1,000 units in 2025 to 4,000 to 5,000 T800 units in 2026, and 30,000 to 50,000 units a year by 2027.

Valuation: Post-money valuation implied by the US$200m Series B led by Henan Investment Group's Huirong Fund and Luxshare Precision, closed April 2026. No public prospectus valuation yet.

02 Core product lines
SA01
First bipedal robot, launched July 2024 at RMB38,500; entry-level developer platform.
SE01
Full-size general-purpose humanoid; 170cm, 55kg, 32 degrees of freedom, 2 m/s walking.
PM01
Lightweight 138cm humanoid; performed the world's first robot front flip; lead commercial model.
T800
Flagship humanoid, 173cm, 450 N·m peak joint torque, priced about RMB180,000 (US$25,000); debuted CES 2026.
Quadruped robots
Four-legged machines for terrain navigation and industrial inspection.

01 Financial performance

FY2025 revenue reached RMB 1.69bn, up about 333% year on year, with net profit of RMB 278m, making Unitree a rare profitable humanoid maker. Revenue grew from RMB 123m in 2022 to RMB 159m (2023) and RMB 392m (2024). Gross margin rose to roughly 60% in 2025 from 44% in 2023. Q1 2026 revenue was RMB 420m (up 68.5%) but net profit fell to RMB 50m (down 47.7%) as price competition compressed margins.

Valuation: Implied IPO valuation at CSRC registration approval; RMB 4.2bn raise for roughly 10% of enlarged capital.

02 Core product lines
Quadruped robots
Go2, B2 and A2 robot dogs for consumer, research and industrial use; over 60% global market share.
Humanoid robots
H1, G1, R1 and H2 full and half-size humanoids; roughly 52% of 2025 revenue.
Actuators and sensors
In-house motors, joints and LiDAR components supplied across its robot platforms.

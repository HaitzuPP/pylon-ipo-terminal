01 Financial performance

FY2025 revenue was US$3.80m, down from US$14.93m in 2024 as Kodiak shifted from pilot freight toward customer-owned driverless deployments. GAAP net loss was roughly US$585.5m, inflated by SPAC, warrant, and stock-based compensation items; diluted loss was US$6.42 per share. Q1 2026 revenue recovered to about US$1.83m, up 74% sequentially. Free cash flow for 2025 was negative US$116.5m against US$94.4m of operating cash burn.

Valuation: Market cap from public trading at roughly US$5.08 per share (stockanalysis.com), down from the US$10.00 SPAC reference price.

02 Core product lines
Kodiak Driver
Proprietary autonomous driving system with redundant hardware and modular SensorPods for driverless trucks.
RoboTrucks (customer-owned)
Kodiak Driver-equipped semi-trucks sold to customers who own and operate their own driverless fleets.
Permian Basin driverless operations
Off-road proppant hauling for Atlas Energy across West Texas and eastern New Mexico.
Highway freight
Autonomous long-haul freight delivery across the US South, ahead of broader on-highway rollout.
Defense autonomy
Adapts the commercial stack to develop and test autonomous capabilities for the US Department of Defense.

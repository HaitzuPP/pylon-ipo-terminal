01 Financial performance

FY2025 revenue rose to RMB 4.89bn from RMB 4.01bn in 2024, as industrial robots and systems grew 31.9% to RMB 4.0bn while core automation components fell 9.0% to RMB 888.5m. Net profit returned to RMB 41.3m attributable (RMB 45.0m total) after an RMB 810m loss in 2024 that was driven partly by goodwill impairment. Operating cash flow swung positive and gearing eased to 78.6%. The A-share valuation is stretched, with a trailing P/E near 300x on thin earnings.

Valuation: A-share (002747.SZ) market cap; A-shares up roughly 89% YTD 2026 on the profit turnaround and humanoid push. The H-shares trade at a wide discount, debuting at HK$12.94 versus the HK$15.36 offer.

03 Core product lines
Industrial robots and intelligent manufacturing systems
Six-axis, SCARA and collaborative robots plus turnkey workcells; the largest domestic brand by shipments in China.
Automation core components and motion control
Servo drives, motors, motion controllers and PLCs; the original CNC and motion-control business.
Trio Motion Technology (UK)
Motion controllers and motion-PLC platforms; acquired 2017.
Cloos (Germany)
Arc-welding robots and welding automation; control acquired 2019 to 2020.
Estun Codroid
Humanoid and collaborative robot joint modules; Codroid 01 and Codroid 02 humanoids released in 2025.

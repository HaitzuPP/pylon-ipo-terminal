01 Financial performance

Revenue rose from RMB 743m in 2023 to RMB 1,325m in 2024 and RMB 2,413m (~US$355m) in 2025, an over-80% CAGR. Gross margin expanded from 17.5% in 2023 to 71.6% in 2025 as software licensing scaled. Licensing revenue grew 42x, from RMB 23m in 2023 to RMB 968m in 2025. The company remains pre-profit, with cash reserves above RMB 10.9bn at end-2025.

Valuation: Market cap at 8 Jul 2026 HK debut; IPO priced at HK$295.60, raising ~HK$5.89bn (US$751m).

03 Core product lines
Mpilot
Mass-production assisted and autonomous driving software for private vehicles, covering highway, urban and parking.
Mpilot Pro
Cost-tuned ADAS and navigation-pilot stack for mainstream mid-range vehicles priced RMB 150k to 350k.
MSD (Momenta Self Driving)
Scalable robotaxi solution powering commercial pilots with Uber, Grab, Mercedes-Benz Lumo and others.
Mpilot Parking
Automated parking software replacing manual parking to improve convenience and safety.
04 Key history
2016
Founded in Beijing by Cao Xudong, Sun Gang, Ren Shaoqing and Xia Yan.
2019
Rolled out Mpilot Parking and highway pilot systems; became early Chinese AD unicorn.
2021
Strategic investment from Mercedes-Benz, Toyota, GM, Bosch and SAIC.
2022
Announced urban NOA solution on NVIDIA DRIVE Orin for scaled commercialisation.
2025
Revenue reached RMB 2,413m with 71.6% gross margin; robotaxi pilots expanded to Abu Dhabi.

2026-07

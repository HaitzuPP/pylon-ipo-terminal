01 Financial performance

Revenue is not disclosed. Since its May 2023 founding, Galbot has raised close to RMB 7bn (about US$1bn) across roughly three years, including a RMB 2.5bn (US$362m) round in March 2026 and over US$300m in December 2025 that set an approximately US$3bn post-money valuation. The company reports thousands of cumulative orders but has released no revenue or margin figures.

Valuation: Post-money after the Dec 2025 raise; reiterated as China's highest-valued unlisted humanoid firm after the Mar 2026 RMB 2.5bn round.

02 Core product lines
Galbot G1
Wheeled semi-humanoid mobile manipulator, 173cm, 47 degrees of freedom, for retail, pharmacy, warehouse and manufacturing tasks.
GraspVLA
End-to-end embodied grasping foundation model pre-trained on billions of synthetic simulation interactions, released Jan 2025.
Unmanned pharmacy and retail deployment
G1 units running 24/7 medication sorting in roughly ten Beijing stores, targeting 100 sites across Beijing, Shanghai and Shenzhen.
Automotive and manufacturing pilots
Partner deployments with Toyota, Hyundai, BAIC, Bosch, CATL, Zeekr and Great Wall Motor.

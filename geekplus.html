01 Financial performance

FY2024 revenue reached RMB 2.41bn, up from RMB 2.14bn in 2023, with an IFRS net loss of about RMB 832m (adjusted net loss RMB 92m). H1 2025 revenue rose 31% to RMB 1.03bn, gross margin improved to 35.1%, and adjusted EBITDA turned positive at RMB 11.6m while adjusted net loss narrowed 94% to RMB 11.9m. International sales were 79.5% of H1 2025 revenue. Full-year 2025 guidance is RMB 3.14bn to RMB 3.18bn revenue with loss narrowing to roughly RMB 10m to RMB 30m.

Valuation: Market cap from stockanalysis.com at share price HK$10.28 on 1.34bn shares; down about 39% from IPO price of HK$16.80.

02 Core product lines
Shelf-to-person AMRs
Goods-to-person picking robots that bring inventory shelves to stationary workers.
Tote-to-person systems
High-density RoboShuttle robots delivering totes for faster storage and retrieval.
Pallet and forklift AMRs
Autonomous forklifts and pallet-to-person robots for bulk and heavy-load handling.
Robot-arm picking
Vision-guided robotic arms (PopPick) for automated item-level order picking.
Sorting robots
AMR-based parcel and order sorting for e-commerce and logistics hubs.
Warehouse software suite
Warehouse execution, robot management and intelligent operations platforms orchestrating fleets.
